Discover Relaxation by the Sea

Welcome to The Striped Umbrella — a full-service spa and resort located just steps away from the Gulf of Mexico and only ten miles east of Crab Key, Florida. Relax with the perfect combination of a quiet sandy beach filled with seabirds, and easy access to top shopping, dining, and entertainment. Leave your laptop at home, bring a book, and enjoy the experience—you may never want to leave!
